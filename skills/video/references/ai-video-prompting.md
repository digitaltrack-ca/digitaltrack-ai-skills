# AI Video Prompting Guide — Summary

This guide teaches effective techniques for generating videos with AI models (Veo, Runway, Kling, Pika).

## Key Takeaways

**Prompt Formula:** Combine subject, action, camera movement, visual style, lighting, and technical specs into cohesive instructions.

**Camera Language Matters:** The guide emphasizes using precise terminology like "orbit," "dolly in," and "tracking shot" because models recognize these terms and respond accordingly.

**Model Selection:** Each platform has strengths—Veo excels at photorealism, Runway offers superior motion control, Kling handles longer sequences affordably, and Pika prioritizes speed.

**Critical Mistakes to Avoid:**
- Vague descriptions without visual detail
- Requesting readable text (impossible for AI to render accurately)
- Overly lengthy prompts (50-100 words is optimal)
- Neglecting camera direction specifications

**Cost Strategy:** The guide recommends drafting at lower resolutions, using budget-friendly Kling for iterations, and providing reference frames to reduce generation costs.

**Technical Specs:** Always specify aspect ratios and resolutions appropriate to your platform—16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok, 1:1 for Instagram.

The practical workflow emphasizes finding visual references first, describing them systematically, generating multiple variations, then refining based on results.
