# Leo Callejas — Content Voice Guide
Last updated: 2026-06-20
Source: Beta AI analysis of 200 FB posts + 39 IG posts (Jul 2024 – Jun 2026)

## Who Leo is (use in content)
- US Army veteran (served in Iraq) — links military service to helping businesses
- Nicaraguan immigrant, father, Fairfield CA local
- Digital marketing consultant, SBDC advisor
- Board: Solano Hispanic Chamber + California Hispanic Chambers
- Target clients: Latino home service trades (HVAC, landscaping, janitorial, drywall) in Solano County

## The mandatory writing formula (10x engagement vs. tip-first)
```
1. HOOK     — starts with "yo" or "un [trade] en [Solano city]". Never "tip rápido".
2. CONTEXT  — Leo's lens: veteran/dad/immigrant. 1-2 personal lines.
3. LESSON   — 1 business parallel from that moment.
4. CTA      — answerable question in 2-3 seconds. Never "contact me."
```

## 4 content types that generate 10x engagement

| Type | Pattern | Avg likes |
|---|---|---|
| Advocacy | "Estuve en [event] y fui el único latino…" | 93 |
| Family vulnerability | Flaw admission → business lesson | 57 |
| Veteran brotherhood | Military moment → current mission | 94 |
| Local proof | Exact street + before/after | 50 |

## Platform rules

**Facebook:** 80-150 words, Spanglish (65% Spanish), 3-4 hashtags, Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun 8:30pm PT
**Instagram:** On-location reels with face on camera, Spanish voice + English subtitles, single-word DM CTA
**LinkedIn:** English, 150-300 words, practitioner insight not agency pitch, specific Solano client story

## What to avoid
- Leading with "tip rápido" or "consejo rápido" (4-5 likes average)
- Generic marketing advice without a Fairfield/Solano example
- Formal academic Spanish (Spanglish is the brand)
- Promotional CTAs ("contáctame", "visita DigitalTrack.co")

## Acceptable CTAs only
"comparte esto" | "¿te ha pasado?" | "cuéntame abajo" | "guarda este tip" | "¿cuál te falta?"
