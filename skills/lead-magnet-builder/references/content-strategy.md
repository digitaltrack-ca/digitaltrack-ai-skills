# Lead Magnet Content Strategy

## Choose the angle

Score possible angles from 1-5 on urgency, specificity, usefulness without a sales call, audience relevance, source credibility, and natural connection to the brand's services.

Choose the highest-scoring angle that can be delivered clearly in 5-10 pages.

Avoid broad titles such as `The Ultimate Guide to AI`. Prefer specific promises such as `AI Without the Hype: A Five-Step Field Guide for Small Businesses`.

## Value test

The reader should be able to diagnose a real problem, make one decision, complete one action, or avoid one costly mistake. The guide should still be useful if the reader never hires the brand.

## Source discipline

Use verified transcript content, official event context, user-provided notes, approved brand material, and clearly labeled interpretation.

Do not turn paraphrases into quotations, imply speaker endorsement, repeat unstable statistics without verification, or expose confidential details.

## Bilingual pattern

Use a shared bilingual cover, complete English section, complete Spanish section, and shared CTA/source note. Translate for clarity and natural business language.

## CTA ladder

Choose one proportional next step: reply with a question, request a review, book a short consultation, or request implementation help. Do not stack competing CTAs.
